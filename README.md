# RPG Game

## Project Description

Welcome to our RPG Game! This project is a thrilling role-playing game designed to provide players with an engaging experience filled with adventure, challenges, and strategic gameplay. Below you will find a comprehensive overview of the game's features.

### Key Features

1. **Monster Drops:**  
   Players will encounter various monsters throughout the game, each with unique drops. Collect rare items, gear, and resources from defeated monsters to enhance your character and prepare for tougher challenges.

2. **Boss Battles:**  
   Face off against formidable bosses that test the skills and strategies you've developed during your journey. Each boss has distinct abilities and requires careful planning to defeat. Can you conquer them all?

3. **Difficulty Settings:**  
   Tailor your gameplay experience with multiple difficulty settings. Whether you are a seasoned player looking for a challenge or a newcomer wanting to enjoy the story, thereâ€™s a difficulty level for everyone!

4. **Death Penalty System:**  
   Our game includes a unique death penalty system to heighten the stakes. When your character falls in battle, there are consequences, but with the right strategies, you can mitigate these effects and continue your adventure!
